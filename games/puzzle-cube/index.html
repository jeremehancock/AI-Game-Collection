Swipe to rotate • Tap buttons to turn

How to Play

View Controls

Touch/Swipe: Drag on the cube to rotate the view in any direction. You can spin it 360° to see all sides.

Mouse: Click and drag to rotate the view.

Face Rotation Buttons

F (Front): Rotate the front face clockwise

B (Back): Rotate the back face clockwise

U (Up): Rotate the top face clockwise

D (Down): Rotate the bottom face clockwise

L (Left): Rotate the left face clockwise

R (Right): Rotate the right face clockwise

Action Buttons

Scramble: Randomly scrambles the puzzle cube with 20 moves

Solve: Automatically solves the puzzle cube by reversing all moves with animation

Reset: Instantly returns the puzzle cube to its solved state

Goal

Rotate the faces until each side of the cube shows a single solid color!
